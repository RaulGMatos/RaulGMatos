Assignment 1 Description and Code

Create a calculator app interface. Do not write any code, just create the interface.

  • Name the project calculator.
  • Including the form, the calculator keypad will have 24 appropriately chosen and named objects. A label that looks like a textbox will be used to display the calculator value.
  • Initials and the words "iPhone Calculator" will appear in the form's title bar.
  • The objects will be approximately colored, aligned, and sized. Be sure the digits, letters, and symbols are easily read; bold and larger font.
  • The initial value for the calculator is 0.
  • When executed, the keypad appears in the center of the screen, and your form cannot be resized.
